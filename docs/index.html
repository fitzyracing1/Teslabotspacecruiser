THE CONCEPT

Built for
Optimus.
Designed for
space.

The TSB-01 places Tesla Optimus humanoid robots at the heart of spacecraft operations — as crew, mechanics, and builders. The standout innovation is the modular hex-grid panel interlock system: standardized panels that connect power, data, and structure with high precision, enabling rapid in-space assembly and repair.

This repository documents the full conceptual design with professional schematics, detailed connection mechanics, and a phased build roadmap.
